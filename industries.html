Industries

Our firm provides services to a wide variety of industries. Our capable staff with years of experience understands the challenges each of these industries face. Below are some of the industries that we service along with a brief description. Please contact us if you are looking for a partner in a field that you don't see listed. Our firm exists to help you reach your unique goals.

We understand the significant business challenges involved in running a professional practice. The need for effective planning is critical. Our experienced team will take the time to understand your unique business and personal needs. In addition to traditional accounting services, we can also support you with:
  • Tax planning and compliance
  • Practice management advice
  • Succession and retirement planning
  • Financial planning
  • Business valuation services
Understanding the diverse aspects of farming and its business cycles is a complex job. Provincial and federal regulation offers a never-ending source of complexity for agricultural operations. Erratic commodity prices and elevated distribution costs can have an immense impact on your bottom line. Our firm can support your farming operation with the financial and business advice that will help it succeed. Our services can assist with:
  • Industry-specific tax planning and compliance services
  • Tax effective business transition planning and implementation
  • Profitability and financial management
  • Assistance in navigating government regulations affecting your practice
No matter what role your organization has in the not-for-profit sector, managing costs, competing for funding, and generating and maintaining membership is a constant challenge. Our firm is able to help your not-for-profit organization handle regulatory change, audits, reviews, tax status issues, financial management and reporting, estate gifts, and more. Types of organizations we support include:
  • Not-for-profit associations
  • Social service agencies
  • Arts organizations
  • Educational institutions
  • Religious organizations
  • Condominium Corporations
We partner with property owners, builders, contractors and investors to help them navigate the challenges of a volatile and evolving market. We offer a range of services, including assistance with:
  • Real estate investment
  • Leasing
  • Commercial and residential real estate
  • Real estate development
  • Multi-residential property
  • Real estate acquisition and disposition
  • Other real estate issues
Our professionals are ready to assist you with everything from starting up to transition planning. We work with a variety of business models, including:
  • Brick-and-mortar retail operations

  • Franchising

  • Operational improvement initiatives

  • Navigating government regulation affecting your industry
