Who We Are

Wilkinson Rogers LLP is a full-service Chartered Professional Accounting Firm with experience & expertise serving small to medium sized owner-managed businesses in London and surrounding areas since 1949. We provide peace of mind to local businesses, individuals and community organizations by creating solutions with:

  • Quality Financial Reporting
  • Business Advisory Services
  • Tax Planning and Compliance

We believe in relationships. For us, each client relationship is a partnership. We believe that our success comes as a result of our clients' success.

Commitment to Excellence

Close personal attention is what our firm takes pride in providing to our clients. We give the assurance that the assistance our clients receive comes from years of training, technical experience and financial acumen, along with our on-going investment in:

  • Continuing Professional Development
  • Technology & Internet Media
  • Business Management & Relationships
